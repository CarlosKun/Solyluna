What is an Independent Living Community?

Our secured Independent Living communities are designed for seniors who want to live at an exclusive resort with gourmet meals, access to healthcare professional as needed, recreational and social activities, housekeeping, and transportation, access to interactive communications with family and friends and a professional trained staff dedicated to serve your outmost needs.
An independent living community may be a good option if:

  • Only minor assistance is needed with activities of daily living.
  • You want a place to live that requires little or no home maintenance and yard work.
  • You want to socialize with your peers and broaden your social network.
  • You want to enjoy activities and meals in the company of others.
